Account Deletion

How to request deletion of your account and data
Your data, your choice. We respect your right to have your account and personal data permanently deleted. This page explains how to request deletion and what happens when you do.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your GlowUp account and all associated data, send an email to:

Send your request to
glowup.skincarepartner@gmail.com

Steps to follow

  1. Send an email to glowup.skincarepartner@gmail.com with the subject line "GlowUp Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the phone number associated with your GlowUp account in the body of the email so we can locate your account.
  3. We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7 business days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account deletion request is processed, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account profile — your phone number, name, and preferences
  • Skin scan history — all photos, analysis results, and AI-generated recommendations
  • Routine data — personalized skincare routines and progress tracking
  • Questionnaire responses — skin type, concerns, and lifestyle answers
  • Analytics data — usage events linked to your account

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
  • If you sign back in after deletion, a new account will be created with no previous history.
  • We may retain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked back to you for product improvement purposes.
  • Deletion requests sent from a different email may require additional verification.

Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process or your data, reach out to us at glowup.skincarepartner@gmail.com.
