1. Who We Are

GlowUp ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application that provides AI-powered skin analysis and personalised skincare recommendations. The app is developed and operated by Rahul Sharma, an individual developer based in India.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at rahul.sharma.sai@gmail.com.

2.3 Photos

When you use the Scan feature, you take or upload a photo of your face. This photo is:

  • Compressed and converted to a data format suitable for AI analysis
  • Transmitted to Google's Gemini API for processing (see Section 4)
  • Never stored by us — the photo itself is not saved to our servers or database; only the resulting analysis scores are saved
  • Temporarily stored on your device as a local file reference, which may be cleared when you update the app or clear device storage

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the GlowUp service:

  • Authenticating you securely via phone OTP
  • Displaying your name and profile in the app
  • Performing AI skin analysis when you initiate a scan
  • Storing your scan history so you can track improvement over time
  • Calculating your usage streak
  • Showing personalised skincare tips and routines based on your profile
We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or use it to train AI models.

4. Third-Party Services

GlowUp uses the following third-party services. By using GlowUp, you agree to their respective privacy practices.

Google Firebase (Authentication & Database)

  • What we share: Your phone number is shared with Firebase Authentication to generate and verify OTPs. Your profile data and scan results are stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Purpose: Secure authentication and cloud data storage
  • Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Gemini API (AI Skin Analysis)

  • What we share: Your face photo (as image data), along with your age range, skin type, and main concern, are sent to Google's Gemini API each time you perform a scan.
  • Purpose: AI-powered skin analysis and scoring
  • Important: As of the date of this policy, Google states that data submitted to the Gemini API is not used to train its models when submitted via the API. Please review Google's Gemini API terms for the most current information.
  • Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Expo / EAS (App Updates)

  • What we share: No personal data. EAS delivers over-the-air app updates.
  • Privacy policy: expo.dev/privacy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your profile and scan results are stored in Google Firebase Firestore, protected by security rules that ensure only you can access your own data.
  • We use HTTPS for all network communication.
  • We do not store face photos on our servers.
  • Your profile data is also stored locally on your device. On rooted devices, locally stored data may be accessible to other apps. We recommend using GlowUp on non-rooted devices.

6. Your Rights Under the DPDPA 2023

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

Right to access — You may request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to correction — You may update your name, skin type, and other profile data directly in the app (Profile screen).

Right to erasure — You may request deletion of your account and all associated data via the Profile screen or by emailing us. We will delete your data within 30 days.

Right to grievance redressal — Contact us at rahul.sharma.sai@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Right to nominate — You may nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity, as permitted under the DPDPA.
