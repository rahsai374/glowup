# GlowUp — 15-Day Facebook + Instagram Content Strategy

**Stage:** Pre-launch (Days 1–7) → Launch (Day 8) → Post-launch growth (Days 9–15)
**Platforms:** Instagram (primary) + Facebook (cross-post all feed content)
**Handle:** @glowupapp
**Audience:** Indian women 18–35, Tier 2/3 cities, Hindi-Hinglish register
**Visual system:** Terracotta (#E07856) on cream (#FFF5EE), Fraunces headings, ambient blur blobs — render via `assets/social-post.html`
**CTA logic:** Days 1–7 → "Link in bio" (Vercel landing page). Day 8+ → "Download" (Play Store link).
**Language:** Primary captions in Hinglish (Roman script Hindi + English). Devanagari lines where they hit harder. Never English-only.

---

## Content Pillars

| # | Pillar | Role | Searchable / Shareable |
|---|--------|------|------------------------|
| 1 | **Skin Problems = Your Problems** | Relatability. Mirror the audience's daily frustrations. | Shareable |
| 2 | **Desi Skincare Knowledge** | Authority. Haldi, multani mitti, neem, aloe — position GlowUp as the layer that tells you *which* nuskha works for *your* skin. | Both |
| 3 | **Product in Action** | Conversion. Show the app doing the thing — scan, score, routine, product check. | Shareable |
| 4 | **Founder / Behind the Scenes** | Trust. Solo Indian founder building for Indian women. | Shareable |
| 5 | **User Education** | Retention. Teach real skincare concepts (skin types, ingredients, routines) — each one ends with "GlowUp tells you this automatically." | Both |

---

## Posting Cadence

- **Feed posts:** 1/day (15 total)
- **Stories:** 2–3/day (polls, quizzes, BTS, countdowns, user questions)
- **Reels:** 5 across 15 days (Days 3, 5, 8, 11, 14) — Reels drive Explore reach
- **Best times (IST):** 11:00 AM (lunch scroll), 7:00 PM (evening scroll), 12:00 PM (Reels for Explore)
