# 7-Day Pre-Launch Meta Post Plan — GlowUp

**Stage:** Pre-launch (app not yet on Play Store)
**Platform:** Instagram + Facebook (@glowupapp)
**CTA:** "Learn More" → Vercel landing page (switch to "Download" on launch day)
**Audience:** Tier 2/3 Indian women, 18–35, Hindi-Hinglish register
**Visual system:** Terracotta (#E07856) on cream (#FFF5EE), Fraunces headings, ambient blur blobs — use `assets/social-post.html` as the creative template
**Assumption:** Day 8 = Play Store launch. Schedule this plan to end the day before the listing goes live.

---

## Content Principles

1. **Bharat-native first.** Lead with desi concerns (dark spots, oiliness, acne scars) and desi remedies (haldi, multani mitti, aloe vera, neem). The app's content is Bharat-native — the posts must match.
2. **Hindi-Hinglish captions.** Write primary captions in Hinglish (Roman script Hindi mixed with English). Add a Hindi (Devanagari) line where it lands harder. English-only captions will feel disconnected from the target audience.
3. **No install CTA yet.** The app isn't on Play Store during this week. Every post drives to the landing page or WhatsApp community. Day 7 (launch-eve) teases tomorrow's availability.
4. **No pricing language.** Never say "free," "no subscription," or commit to any pricing model in post copy.
5. **Relatable > aspirational.** Show real skin concerns, not flawless-skin fantasy. The hook is "finally, koi toh samjhe meri skin ko" (finally, someone understands my skin), not "get perfect skin."

## Stories Strategy (Daily, All 7 Days)

Run parallel Stories content every day alongside the feed post:

- **Day 1–2:** Polls and quizzes — "Tumhari sabse badi skin problem kya hai?" / "Kaunsa gharelu nuskha use karti ho?"
- **Day 3–4:** Behind-the-scenes app screenshots, "sneak peek" energy
- **Day 5:** Q&A sticker — "GlowUp ke baare mein kuch poochna hai?"
- **Day 6:** "This or That" — "Morning routine pehle ya night routine?" / "Sunscreen daily ya kabhi kabhi?"
- **Day 7:** Countdown sticker + "Kal milte hain" hype
